Security

Enterprise Security Architecture

Purpose-built for regulated industries and zero-trust environments

Authentication & Identity

OAuth 2.0 with PKCE flow — tokens never exposed in URLs. Supports SAML, OIDC, and Native TD credentials. Encrypted token storage on Desktop and Mobile. Automatic session refresh before expiration.

Server-Side Sandbox

All AI code execution happens server-side in a restricted sandbox. No filesystem access. No arbitrary network access. Credentials managed server-side, never stored in browsers. This is the architecture security teams want to see.

Network Audit & Monitoring

Full Network Audit Log with temporal filtering (1h to 30d), domain/IP/port filtering, and real-time statistics. IP Allowlist with fixed static outbound IPs. See exactly what the AI accessed, when, and where.

Policy-Based Access Control

Policy-based permissions inherited from Treasure Data Console. All queries, segments, and API calls execute through the user's own credentials. Same governance model your security team already approved for TD.

Key Message for IT Buyers

Treasure AI Studio inherits the security posture of the Treasure Data platform. If they've approved TD, there's no new vendor risk assessment needed. Offer a joint security review call with their CISO to walk through the architecture.

Battle Cards

Objection Handling

Common pushback and how to respond

"We already have ChatGPT / Copilot — why do we need another AI?"
Response: ChatGPT doesn't know your customers. It can't query your CDP, build segments from your data, or activate audiences to your channels. Treasure AI Studio is AI that's connected to your customer data — the difference between a general assistant and a data-powered marketing co-pilot. Ask them: "Can ChatGPT tell you which of your customers are about to churn based on your actual purchase data?"
"Our security team won't approve another AI tool."
Response: Treasure AI Studio inherits the same enterprise security posture as the Treasure Data platform they've already approved. Console SSO, role-based access, same data residency controls. No new vendor to approve — it's part of the platform they already trust. Offer to do a joint security review call with their CISO.
"We tried AI and the outputs weren't accurate enough."
Response: Generic AI hallucinates because it's guessing. Treasure AI Studio is grounded in your actual data — it runs real queries against your CDP and returns verified results. The Skills architecture means it follows defined, tested workflows rather than making things up. Offer a live demo with their data to prove accuracy.
"We want to wait for the custom skill marketplace."
Response: The pre-installed TD Skills cover the most common use cases today — segmentation, activation, analytics, reporting. Customers who start now build organizational muscle with AI and will be first movers when the marketplace launches. The marketplace extends value; it doesn't gate it.
"What about customers who can't use Anthropic models?"
Response: For customers requiring non-Anthropic models (e.g., UMG, SoftBank), AI Agent Foundry remains available and fully supported. We're actively working on enabling non-Anthropic model support in Treasure AI Studio. Foundry is not going away — it serves a different need.
"How is this different from the old Treasure Studio?"
Response: Treasure AI Studio is a ground-up rebuild — new architecture (Claude Agent SDK), new interfaces (web + desktop + mobile + voice), new security model (console SSO), and new UI. Treasure Studio is being renamed to Treasure Work and will serve as an internal innovation lab. Treasure AI Studio is the future — SLA-supported, customer-facing, enterprise-ready.
"How much does this cost? What's the pricing model?"
Response: Treasure AI Studio uses a credit-based consumption model with three AI model tiers: Pro (Claude Opus 4.6) for complex reasoning, Balanced (Claude Sonnet 4.6) for everyday tasks, and Fast (Claude Haiku 4.5) for quick lookups. Users switch models mid-conversation with one click to optimize cost. Most daily tasks run on Balanced or Fast, keeping consumption predictable. No per-seat licensing — teams pay for what they use.

Demo Playbook

Recommended Demo Flow

A 15-minute demo script that converts

1Open with Context (2 min)

Goal: Frame the problem before showing the solution.

  • "How long does it take your team to get an answer from your customer data today?"
  • "How many people in your org can directly access your CDP?"
  • Let them tell you the pain — then say: "Let me show you what it looks like when anyone on your team can ask your data a question and get an answer in 30 seconds."
2Live Query — Instant Insight (3 min)

Goal: Show the "magic moment" — natural language to real data.

  • Type a question relevant to their industry: "Show me customers who purchased in Q4 but haven't engaged in 60 days"
  • Point out: real data, real query execution, real results — not a canned demo
  • Key feature: Click the expandable tool call card to reveal the exact SQL query the agent ran — builds instant trust with technical buyers
  • Demonstrate message editing to refine a query, and mid-generation stopping if you see it going wrong
  • Show the file viewers: charts, tables, exportable formats
3Segmentation & Activation (4 min)

Goal: Demonstrate the full workflow from question to action.

  • "Now take those at-risk customers and create a re-engagement segment"
  • Show how Studio builds the segment with proper conditions — point out the validate-segment skill that checks for errors before committing
  • Show the journey canvas visualization for the activation flow — interactive, clickable stages
  • "Activate this to Salesforce / Google Ads / your preferred channel"
  • Key message: insight to action in one conversation
4Multi-Interface & Mobile (2 min)

Goal: Differentiate from desktop-only tools.

  • Show the mobile app — voice input a question
  • "Imagine your CMO checking campaign performance from their phone before a board meeting"
  • Quick desktop app overview — native experience, offline-ready
5Enterprise & Security (2 min)

Goal: Address the buyer (IT/Security) who's in the room but hasn't spoken yet.

  • Show console SSO login flow — highlight PKCE OAuth (tokens never exposed in URLs)
  • Demonstrate role-based permissions — "Marketing sees marketing data, not finance data"
  • Show the Network Audit Log — filter by domain, IP, port, and time range (1h to 30d)
  • Mention IP Allowlist with fixed static outbound IPs and the server-side sandbox: "All code runs on our servers, never on the client device"
6Close & Next Steps (2 min)

Goal: Create urgency and define next actions.

  • "What we showed today took 5 minutes. How long does this take your team today?"
  • Propose a pilot: "Let's connect Studio to your data and run a real use case in 2 weeks"
  • Offer to send a tailored ROI analysis based on their team size and current workflow

Strategic Context

Product Portfolio & Customer Playbook

How our product lineup is evolving — and how to position each product by customer type

Product Portfolio Consolidation

We're simplifying our product lineup so every rep has one clear recommendation. Here's what's changing:

Treasure Studio → Treasure Work

The product formerly known as "Treasure Studio" has been renamed Treasure Work. It continues as our internal innovation lab — not a customer-facing sales motion.

MSA Retiring — End of May

MSA capabilities are being absorbed into Treasure AI Studio. No action needed from customers — the transition is seamless and all functionality carries over.

AI Agent Foundry — No Change

Foundry remains as-is. It continues to serve customers who require non-Anthropic models or have existing custom agent deployments.

Customer Migration Playbook

Different customers need different motions. Use this playbook to tailor your approach:

New Customers

Lead with Treasure AI Studio. For any new prospect that can use Anthropic models, Studio is your default recommendation — it's the primary, SLA-supported AI experience. Position it as the fastest path from data to insight to action.

Existing Foundry Customers

Expand, don't displace. No forced migration — let them continue current usage until all custom agents have been migrated to Skills. In the meantime:

  • Showcase Studio's new capabilities as additional use cases beyond what they're doing in Foundry today
  • If they're comfortable running both apps, encourage them to try Skills in Treasure AI Studio — it's the natural upgrade path
  • Frame it as "getting more value from the credits you already own"

Non-Anthropic Model Customers

Foundry remains the right fit — for now. Accounts like UMG and SoftBank that require non-Anthropic models should stay on Foundry until non-Anthropic model support ships in Studio. Do not position Studio as a Foundry replacement in these accounts.

The One-Liner

"Treasure AI Studio is where every customer starts. Foundry is where specific model requirements live." When in doubt, lead with Studio — it's simpler to sell, faster to deploy, and backed by full SLA support.
